ARTDES 115 Midterm

For ARTDES 115 (Drawing Visualization), we were tasked to create a 'fusion' object, with the options being a household item, a sculpture, or an art toy. As I've always enjoyed character design immensely, I chose the art toy, with the theme being people, fruit and fruit-based weapons. The idea was that they would be a collectible series.

ARTDES 115 Final Project

The final for ARTDES 115 (Drawing Visualization) was to create a twist on a myth, fable, or fairytale as a comic where no dialogue was permitted. I chose Pandora's Box as the inspiration for my comic, featuring Pandora as an exhausted computer science student, and the evils released are mysterious errors (a virus?) that break out of the computer.
